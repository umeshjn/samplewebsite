Why Tech Skills are the most Important skills of 21st Century?

Technological advancements in the last 10 years have changed the way we communicate, the way we buy things, the way we do banking, the way we learn, etc. This is only going to get better going forward. Knowing technological skills will be important and basic as knowing how to read and write. Irrespective of the job our kids are going to be doing in the future Computer skills will play a very big role in their growth and success.

Courses

We have curated the best courses in the world to teach topics ranging various skills which we consider the most important ones for the creators of tomorrow.

Coding

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, software developer jobs are expected to grow 17% from 2014 till 2024. They categorize this growth as ‘much faster’ than the average rate among other professions

Robotics

The world is going to see more and more robots in every field and the demand for engineers who can build better robots will only grow. These courses will teach the kids to be prepared for the future world of robots.

Game Development

Video Games Industry Revenue is expected to hit $180 billion in 2018. Opportunity size is growing bigger and bigger. Creating games makes kids more creative and also helps them grow their logical thinking.

Design

Every man made object is designed and created by someone. The process of designing and seeing it coming to life is one of the best learning methodology since ancient times. But today our education systems have forgotten that.
